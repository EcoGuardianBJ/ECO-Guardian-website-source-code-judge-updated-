SmartLeaf is an AI-powered system that uses the HuskyLens camera to analyze leaf images and detect signs of disease, stress, or nutrient deficiency.

The HuskyLens captures a leaf image and sends it into an AI model. The AI checks colour, texture, patterns, and disease-like spots to determine the leafâ€™s condition.

SmartLeaf currently uses HuskyLens for image detection and a Raspberry Pi Zero 2 W to process the data and send the results to a Telegram bot using WiFi.

The Raspberry Pi works as the communication brain. It receives detection results, processes them, and sends real-time notifications to Telegram.

AI can detect tiny changes that humans usually miss, especially at early stages of disease. This helps with faster action and reduces tree loss.

SmartLeaf aims to assist farmers, environmental officers, PERHILITAN, and students by offering a fast and reliable AI-driven plant health diagnosis tool.
